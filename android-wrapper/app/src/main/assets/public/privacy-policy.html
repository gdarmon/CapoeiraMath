Privacy Policy for Darmon Capoeira

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Darmon Capoeira is a multiplication practice game for children. The app is designed to help practice multiplication facts with capoeira-themed rewards and encouragement.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information. The app does not require an account, name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or payment information.

Progress Stored on the Device

The app stores game progress locally on the device, such as practice attempts, correct answers, streaks, hints used, and earned energy points. This information stays on the device and is not sent to our servers.

Internet and Video Rewards

The app uses internet access to show short capoeira reward videos from YouTube after correct answers. When YouTube content is loaded or opened, Google and YouTube may receive information according to their own privacy policies and account settings, including Family Link settings. YouTube content is provided by YouTube and is not controlled by Darmon Capoeira.

Text-to-Speech and Sound

The app may use Android text-to-speech to read questions and encouragement aloud. This is handled by the device. The app does not record audio.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended to be usable by children, including children under 13, with parent or guardian supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third Parties

The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or social login. The only external service used by the app is YouTube/Google for reward videos and links.

Data Deletion

Local progress can be deleted by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app from the device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if the app changes. The updated policy will be posted at this same location.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing for Darmon Capoeira.
